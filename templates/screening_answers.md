# Screening Answers Bank

High-value projects where AI improves business outcomes, especially when the work
benefits from both strategic taste and practical automation.

## What makes you different?
A rare combination of:
- strong aesthetic judgment,
- AI-native creative execution,
- business-facing AI systems thinking.

Low-budget work, generic deliverables, and roles that are really hiring a traditional
full-time software developer under a vague AI label.
